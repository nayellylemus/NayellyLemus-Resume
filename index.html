When I sent myself to do something, I am determined to see it through. I aspire to become a great teacher that children can look up to. I plan on earning a teaching certificate and intend to focus on the children that have been left behind as a result of disabilities.

During my high school years, I spent 2 years volunteering with children in an afterschool program. I worked with them everyday helping them with homework or reading. Generally, I would work with students that were having the same problem so that I was able to help them all at once. Furthermore, since it was a smaller group of kids I would work with, they felt more comfortable asking questions to better understand the subject. It was a new experience for me to do this and I enjoyed every moment of it. Helping these kids showed me that everyone learns at their own pace and their method of learning can be different from others. This encouraged me to find different techniques to help those who struggled to grasp the subject clearly.

My goal is to be someone that ensure no student gets left behind. One of the main paths I intend to take to achieve this goal is to focus on students that have disabilities since these students are more likely to fall behind as a result of not having the right material.
